WHO AM I?

Born in California and raised in Texas, Kevin Rosas has a passion for software creation and film production.

In the tech side of things, Kevin is knowledgable in C# and Python programming. He has also picked up HTML/CSS/Javascript and hopes to one day become an experienced Web Developer who can help create websites for people who have no clue how to even begin. He posts some of his work on Github.

Kevin has spent a majority of his time working on the creation of film-related content. He graduated from Orange Coast College in May 2022 with an associates degree in Film. He has since delved into all aspects of film production to further understand and aid others on his team.
